About us

“Nerd Today Boss Tomorrow” is a group of Young future entrepreneurs. Consists of business , computer science and technical students who meet to discuss entrepreneurship topics and business trends. Subjects covered can include how to create your own blue ocean business model, content strategy, creation, and distribution for your brand on social media, how to run a successful startup, how to bring that entrepreneurship spark in you ,how to hire the best employees, and other current issues encountered by new entrepreneurs. We put efforts to bring the most value to the entrepreneurs, marketers and businesses of the world. We want to empower them to create massive wealth, and entrepreneurial success, without sacrificing integrity or ethics.
